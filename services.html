Our Services

Integrated stratigraphic services including high-resolution biostratigraphy, sedimentology, and more.

Biostratigraphy

High-resolution Biostratigraphy, evaluating microfossils to date sedimentary rocks and build accurate stratigraphic frameworks for exploration.

Sedimentology
