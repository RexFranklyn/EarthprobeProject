Who We Are

Discover the history, expertise, and vision driving our excellence.

A Legacy of Geological Excellence.

Earthprobe Nigeria Limited was incorporated in Nigeria in 1997. It is a petroleum geological consultancy company providing stratigraphic services for all types of exploration and production applications.

The company was established by time-tested and experienced oil industry professionals to provide high quality services for the oil and gas industry. Our team members have excellently handled varied assignments for several clients in Nigeria and other parts of Africa.

We have also established strong technical relationships with experienced companies overseas. The company intends to provide the leading edge in stratigraphic and geological consultancy in Nigeria and the rest of the West Coast of Africa.

Company History

The petroleum industry constitutes Nigeria's economic bedrock today. Available statistics suggest that over 90% of the country’s external earnings are via the sale of petroleum products. Expectably, the industry has been extensively ventured into by various international, and more recently indigenous firms, and remains the most important national economic activity currently.

Their vast technical capabilities notwithstanding, petroleum companies require an extensive range of support services which are essentially consultancy based. These services range from pre-drilling operations, syn-drilling and post-drilling operations. These needs outlined foregoing, in addition to a desire to further enhance indigenous capacity in the petroleum industry in Nigeria motivated the establishment of the Earthprobe Nigeria Limited in 1997. To technically support and compliment the efforts of the petroleum industry in Nigeria. Biostratigraphy is the discipline upon which the company was founded, and this continues to form the backbone of its present day consulting activities and has since developed and expanded steadily to become a market leader in the field.

Earthprobe Nigeria Limited is a leading provider of biostratigraphic service for all types of exploration and production applications. We have teams of experienced in-house specialist biostratigraphers with expertise spanning across all disciplines of micropaleontology, nannopaleontology, and palynology. Our team members have been in the business for years and satisfactorily handled various assignments for reputable clients within and outside Nigeria. We are equipped to carry out all your biostratigraphic projects in our office/laboratory.

Why use Biostratigraphy?

  • Age Determination
  • Correlation
  • Calibrating seismic picks and providing stratigraphic control where seismic is poor.
  • Interpreting paleoenvironments and paleobathymetry.
  • Helps to identify faults, unconformities, facies change and pinch out
  • Important tool in sequence stratigraphic analysis.
  • Reservoir characterization
  • Source Rock Evaluation
  • Real-time drilling/monitoring support and geo-steering.
