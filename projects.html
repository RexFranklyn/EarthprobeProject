Experience

Individual experience covers decades of working for major oil companies and other consulting firms.

Over 200 wells analyzed in Nigeria (Niger Delta, Deep Offshore, Dahomey, Chad and Anambra Basins). Sudan (Melut, Muglad Basins).

Experience spans Cretaceous to Recent sediments in the onshore, shallow offshore and deep offshore Nigeria.

Strong proficiency especially in the use of foraminifera, nannofossil, palynomorphs (dinocysts, miospores and acritachs), ostracods and macrofossils within the Cretaceous and Tertiary periods.

We believe our strength lies in the depth of our experience and in the diversity of Individual formal education and work history.
