Sample Storage and Administration

Earthprobe Nigeria Limited will provide all materials and resources to manage sample administration facility and services.

  • Sample storage
  • Sample handling, splitting and packaging
  • Sample Washing
  • Sample inventorization
  • Sample transportation/ dispatch to partner, contractors and export
  • Data Storage (seismic, well tapes, logs, maps etc)
